About Me

My interest in web development began in 5th grade when I created my first website. Being able to turn lines of code into something I could interact with online felt amazing. I continued this passion during high school and created a private server for a popular online game. Since the game used a programming language I was not familiar in, I learned how to effectively sift through an abundance of online resources and debugged issues as they came up. The experience was challenging, but gratifying when everything finally worked.

Following in the business oriented footsteps of my family, I graduated from UC Irvine with a BA in Business Administration and an emphasis in marketing. During my career, I took on very analytical roles and was fortunate to work with some incredible people who shared their expertise. I learned how to develop logical strategies, effectively communicate with clients and prioritize projects to meet deadlines. As I searched for new ways to optimize my work, I began to wish I knew enough programming to build a custom solution. This made me realize how much I missed the problem-solving nature of programming and the feeling of fulfillment when a project finally works after going through so many issues.

After much consideration, I made the tough decision to quit my job to focus my full efforts on learning programming. Today, I can confidently say that it was all worth it! I am now constantly being challenged and the learning never stops. I can pair my business experience and technical ability to create working solutions for the toughest of problems. I get to collaborate with great minds to collectively improve our skills and develop amazing applications. The industry is never stagnant and I am excited for what comes next.

Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I look forward to connecting with you and learning more about your goals.
