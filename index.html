About Me

Hi there! You might be wondering how do you pronounce my name. It's much simplier than it seems!

Jaytrin Apinchapong

[ Jey-trin Uh-pinch-uh-pong ]

Origin: Born and raised in Southern California.

noun

1. A driven and effective web developer.

2. A positive team-player who enjoys solving problems.

Programming is fun for me! I really enjoy the challenge of breakdown down complex problems into feasible solutions while sharing knowledge and collaborating with my peers. I cannot wait to learn more about your company's goals and how we can achieve them as a team.

My roots in web development began in 5th grade when I created my very first website. As the great organizing consultant and author Marie Kondo would say, it sparked joy in me. I carried over this fascination with programming into high school and created a custom private server for a popular online game to play with my friends. At the time, I was not familiar with the technologies the game used, but piecing together different online resources and debugging became a fun game in itself. The experience was challenging, but the moment the game went live, you couldn't wipe the smile off my face.

As college came around, I decided to follow in the footsteps of my family and pursue a career in business. I graduated cum laude from UC Irvine with a BA in Business Administration and an emphasis in Marketing. During my career, I took on analytical roles and was very fortunate to work with some incredible people. I learned how to effectively communicate with clients, develop logical strategies and properly implement large projects. The work itself was interesting, but I always felt that something was missing. After much consideration, I made the tough decision to quit my job and focus my full efforts on programming. Looking back, I am glad that I decided to take a leap of faith and pursue my passion.
